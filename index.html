On the trail of the Kavikāmadhenu

A critical edition of Subhūticandra's commentary on Amarakoṣa 1.0.1

Charles Li

This digital edition is the supplementary material to the article On the trail of the Kavikāmadhenu published in Journal of Sanskrit Studies 1. That article contained a critical edition of the commentary on Amarakoṣa 1.0.1, the maṅgalācaraṇa.
